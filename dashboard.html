Key Insights

  • Across all 805 Done issues, the median cycle time (In Progress → Done) is 4.04 days, with a mean of 4.43 days. The 85th percentile is 8.01 days and 95th percentile is 9.16 days.
  • 46 issues spent more than half a day blocked. Among those, the average blocked time was 3.7 days. Reducing blocked time is one of the highest-leverage improvements.
  • Cycle times improved over time: the first 4 sprints averaged 4.5-day median vs 4.2 days in the last 4 (~6% improvement).
  • Average throughput: first 4 sprints = 67.0 issues/sprint, last 4 sprints = 62.0 issues/sprint.
  • Flow efficiency (active work / lead time): median 70%. Lead time includes backlog wait before work starts. Higher efficiency means less waiting. World-class teams target >40%.
  • Median lead time (9.11d) exceeds median cycle time (4.04d) by 5.1 days, meaning issues sit in Backlog for a median of ~5.1 days before work begins.
